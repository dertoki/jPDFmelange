Internationalization:

Currently the English and German translations are finished.
If you like your language to be supported, please make a translation of the file
 <jPDFmelange/resources/MelangeMessages.properties> and send it to the author <toki104400 at users.berlios.de> .

 <MelangeMessages.properties> is a part of  <jPDFmelange.jar>. Simply open the jar file with your standard zip program to get a copy.
