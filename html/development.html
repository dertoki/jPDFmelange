Compilation:
You need the following GPL'ed or free libraries to create the code:

  • iText-2.1.0 also available at sourceforge.org
  • jPedal3.45b37 also available at sourceforge.org
  • bcprov-jdk at bouncycastle.org
Get the sources of jPDFmelage via CVS at <developer.berlios.de/cvs>

Internationalization:
Currently the English and German translations are finished. If you like your language to be supported, please make a translation of the file <jPDFmelange/resources/MelangeMessages.properties> and send it to the author <toki104400 at users.berlios.de>.
<MelangeMessages.properties> is a part of <jPDFmelange.jar>. Simply open the jar file with your standard zip program to get a copy.
